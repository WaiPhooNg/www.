Some basic information you should know

Turkey is a large peninsula that bridges the countinents of Europee and Asia. Istanbul is the largest city in Turkey and there has 64 streets and 4,000 shops.If you choose to go there, we're sure there will be alot of fun!!! Also, Turkey has a very diverse and intersting culture that is a blend of various elements of the Oğuz Turkic and Anatolian, Ottoman (which was itself a continuation of both Greco-Roman and Islamic cultures), and Western culture and traditions which started with the Westernization of the Ottoman Empire and continues today. Turkey Cuisine

Turkish cuisine is an interesting cuisine, it's a mix between Italian, Greece, Albania and French cuisines along with some Ottoman cuisine mixed in. The cuisine follows the legacy of the Ottoman cuisine and certainly does it very well.
