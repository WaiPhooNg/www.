Travel Advice

Some travelling advice for when you go to China

Img Source : WallpaperAccess.com

The best time to visit China is in Spring or Autumn where the weather is warm and pleasant and most of the tourists and visitors aren't here. During this period most of the landmarks and historical sites are not crowded meaning that you can enjoy a more private time when visiting landmarks and historical sites. Another thing we advise you to do when visiting China is that you should visit the various shrines and temples around China as meditating or praying there is often said to give you good luck.


Basic History 1

Image source : wikipedia commons

China was ruled by various dynasties much of its history. The first dynasty is believed to be the Xia dynasty which formed somewhere around 2250 BC. The Shang or Yin dynasty gained power around the 14th century BC. The Han Dynasty, which lasted over 400 years from 206 BC to 220 AD, was one of the most influential in China's history. Much of the culture today was created during the Han Dynasty. Later famous dynasties, like the Song and the Tang, continued to refine the culture and their government that ruled over 100 million people.

Basic History 2

In the early 1900s the people of China began to want reform. Sun Yat-sen created the Chinese Nationalist Peoples Party, also called the KMT or Kuomintang. After Sun Yat-sen died, Chiang Kai-shek became leader of the party. However, Chiang turned on the leaders of the CCP, the communist party, and had many of them killed. The Chinese Civil War broke out between the KMT and the communists. Which resulted in the communists winning.

Chinese cuisine

Chinese cuisine is an important part of Chinese culture, Chinese cuisine is usually very complex and hard to cook and prepare but we ensure you the taste of the food is worth it! There's a lot of different dishes to try in China from fried rice to roasted duck, if you wanna experience Chinese food you should ask a local there for more information as there are also various region exclusive dishes there.
