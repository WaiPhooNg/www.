Basic History 2

Italy became the birthplace of the Italian Renaissance in the 1400s. Artists such as Leonardo da Vinci and Michelangelo excelled during this time period.Much of Italy desired to become one country in the 1800s. Italy became a constitutional monarchy and a united, sovereign country in 1871.Benito Mussolini became Italy's dictator in 1922. He was the dictator of Italy, which he turned into a fascist regime. During World War II, he supported the Axis Powers of Germany and Japan. Mussolini was dismissed from power when they lost the war. Italy became a republic in 1946.
