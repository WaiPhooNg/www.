Basic History 2

In the 1500s Britain began to expand its empire around the world in various places. After defeating the Spanish Armada in 1588, The British became the world's dominant sea power. Britain first grew into the Far East and India and then to the Americas. In the early 1800s the UK defeated France in the Napoleonic Wars and became the supreme European power. But after 2 world wars the UK became weaker and weaker over time, they eventually lost all of their colonies and ended up in the place they are currently in now. The United Kingdom declined as a world power in the 1900s. It lost control of more colonies and was weakened by World War I. The British played a significant role in world history, taking the lead in developing democracy and advancing literature and science.
