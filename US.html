Basic History 2

After a few decades of independence the Americans got into another war with the British forces once again in the War of 1812. The white house got burned down by the British forces and America was heavily dishonored after the war. After the war of 1812 another terrible event happened to the USA where a brutal civil war occured between the pro-slavery forces of the American south fighting the American northen forces.
